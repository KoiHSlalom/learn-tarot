# The Chariot — VII

**Keywords:** willpower, determination, control, victory

**Upright:** Drive and focused intention moving you forward. Harnessing opposing forces toward a goal.

**Reversed:** Lack of direction, aggression, or feeling out of control. Are you forcing an outcome instead of steering toward it?

**Journal prompt:** What am I pushing toward right now — and am I steering, or just barrelling forward?
