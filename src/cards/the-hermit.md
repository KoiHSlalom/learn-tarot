# The Hermit — IX

**Keywords:** solitude, introspection, inner guidance, wisdom

**Upright:** Withdrawal to find truth within. A period of quiet reflection, seeking answers through inner knowing rather than outside noise.

**Reversed:** Isolation, withdrawal from community, or refusing to share hard-won wisdom.

**Journal prompt:** What do I need to sit quietly with right now instead of seeking outside answers?
