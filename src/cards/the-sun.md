# The Sun — XIX

**Keywords:** joy, vitality, clarity, success

**Upright:** Warmth, clarity, and a sense of wholeness. Things are going well or becoming clear — celebrate and let yourself shine.

**Reversed:** Temporary cloud over your joy, or needing to bring some of that outward shine inward first.

**Journal prompt:** Where in my life am I experiencing or could invite more genuine joy right now?
