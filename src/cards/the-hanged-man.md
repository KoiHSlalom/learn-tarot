# The Hanged Man — XII

**Keywords:** suspension, surrender, new perspective, letting go

**Upright:** Pause willingly. A new viewpoint emerges only when you stop struggling and let things be still for a moment.

**Reversed:** Stalling, martyrdom, or delayed decisions that are overdue.

**Journal prompt:** What would I see differently if I stopped trying to push forward and simply paused?
