# Justice — XI

**Keywords:** fairness, truth, cause and effect, clarity

**Upright:** A time for honest assessment, accountability, and balanced decision-making. What is fair will come to light.

**Reversed:** Unfairness, avoidance of accountability, or distorted thinking clouding judgment.

**Journal prompt:** Where in my life am I being called to be more honest — with myself or with others?
