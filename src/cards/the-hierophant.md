# The Hierophant — V

**Keywords:** tradition, guidance, belief systems, conformity

**Upright:** Established wisdom, mentorship, and structured learning. A card of seeking guidance from trusted traditions or teachers.

**Reversed:** Questioning dogma, breaking conventions, or rigid thinking. Where are you following rules that no longer serve you?

**Journal prompt:** Who or what tradition has shaped my values most? Do those values still fit?
