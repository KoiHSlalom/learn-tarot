# The Empress — III

**Keywords:** abundance, nurturing, creativity, nature

**Upright:** Fertility, sensuality, and creative flowering. A time to nurture yourself and others, and to trust that what you tend will grow.

**Reversed:** Creative block, neglect, over-dependence. Check whether you are caring for yourself as well as others.

**Journal prompt:** Where in my life am I cultivating something that needs more patience and care?
