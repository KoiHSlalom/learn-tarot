# The Tower — XVI

**Keywords:** upheaval, revelation, sudden change, chaos

**Upright:** A sudden collapse of something built on shaky ground. Uncomfortable, but ultimately clearing the way for something more true.

**Reversed:** Avoiding a necessary collapse, or the disruption happening internally rather than externally.

**Journal prompt:** What in my life has been built on a shaky foundation that may need to come down?
