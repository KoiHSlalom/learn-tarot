# Judgement — XX

**Keywords:** reflection, reckoning, awakening, renewal

**Upright:** A call to honest self-appraisal and rising to your higher self. Forgiving yourself and others to move forward.

**Reversed:** Self-doubt, refusing to hear the call, or harsh self-judgment blocking growth.

**Journal prompt:** What part of myself am I being called to forgive or to claim more fully?
