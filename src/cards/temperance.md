# Temperance — XIV

**Keywords:** balance, patience, moderation, flow

**Upright:** Finding the middle path. Blending opposites with care, moving steadily rather than rushing, and trusting the process.

**Reversed:** Imbalance, excess, or rushing a process that needs more time.

**Journal prompt:** Where in my life do I need to slow down and find a more balanced pace?
