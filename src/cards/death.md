# Death — XIII

**Keywords:** endings, transformation, transition, release

**Upright:** A necessary ending that clears the way for something new. This card rarely means literal death — it means change is inevitable and welcome.

**Reversed:** Resistance to change, stagnation, or clinging to something that has already ended.

**Journal prompt:** What am I holding on to that I know has already run its course?
