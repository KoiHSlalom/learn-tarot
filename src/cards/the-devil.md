# The Devil — XV

**Keywords:** bondage, materialism, shadow, addiction

**Upright:** Patterns, habits, or attachments that feel hard to escape. The chains are often looser than they appear — awareness is the first step to freedom.

**Reversed:** Breaking free, releasing attachments, reclaiming personal power.

**Journal prompt:** What habit or pattern do I keep returning to even though I know it doesn't serve me?
