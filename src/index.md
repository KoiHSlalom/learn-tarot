# Learn Tarot

A small, local-first site to help beginners learn and practice Tarot — one card at a time.

## Get started

- [Lessons](/lessons/lesson-1/) — five short lessons from the basics through your first spreads
- [Card Library](/cards/) — Explore the full tarot deck: 78 cards — 22 Major Arcana and 56 Minor Arcana (four suits of 14 cards each). Click through to read meanings and journal prompts.
- [Practice](/practice/) — draw a daily card and keep a private journal in your browser
